How it works

Get Your COOP Items in 3 Easy Steps

Place an order, get notified, and pick up at the COOP.

1. Browse & Order

Submit your request and join the queue. Your dashboard shows “You’re #X in queue.”

2. You’re Next

We’ll notify you when you’re one away. Prepare to go to the counter with your QR.

3. Pick Up & Complete

Show your QR at the counter, claim/pay, and you’re done. Your order is marked Completed.
